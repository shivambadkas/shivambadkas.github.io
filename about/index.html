About Me

Hello and welcome! I am a recent graduate of the University of Toronto where I double majored in Statistics and Global Health and minored in Mathematics. A major theme of my education was the way in which climate change would affect human populations around the globe. I am interested in using statistics and data science to highlight the different ways in which climate change will affect the health of human populations around the globe. In addition, I am also interested in ways how we as individuals can reduce our impact on the environment.

My hobbies include photography (digital and film), reading (fiction, nonfiction, and editorials), listening to music, cooking, gardening, and playing tennis! I also love going on drives and blasting music! If you want to reach out to me and have a conversation feel free to do so!

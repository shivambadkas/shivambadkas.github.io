Indian Authors and Literature

April 15, 2022
review opinion literature politics

Since Covid-19 began, I started reading fiction again after negating it for essentially my entire undergraduate career. I started reading more Indian authors and some important works of the Indian Independence Movement which achieved its goal with the newly created Indian Republic in 1947. Reading magazine articles and essays and primary works astounded me as to how really how historically significant the movement was. In particular, it was interesting to see how the various leaders organized their respective communities into unifying faith, class, creed, and gender against the British Empire, perhaps a first in the world.
